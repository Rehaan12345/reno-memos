SUBJECT: Reno Tahoe Airport Authority Board of Trustees Appointments Update 
This memo is intended to provide an update regarding the City of Reno’s upcoming 
appointments to the Reno Tahoe Airport Authority (RTAA) Board of Trustees. 
The City has received resignations from Edward Ableser and Kitty Jung, resulting in two 
vacancies on the RTAA Board of Trustees which must be filled by the Reno City Council. 
Because both trustees resigned prior to the completion of their terms, Council will be asked to 
appoint individuals to fill the remainder of the unexpired terms, rather than new full terms. In 
addition to the two vacant City of Reno positions, there is currently a third vacancy on the board 
which must be filled by the Reno Sparks Convention and Visitor’s Authority board.  
The City’s board, commission, and committee application portal for the two City of Reno 
vacancies is currently open and will remain open through 5:00 PM Monday, April 6, 2026. 
Following the closure of the application period, staff anticipate bringing an appointment item 
forward for Council’s consideration at the April 22, 2026 Reno City Council meeting. There are 
currently 34 applications on file for the two vacant positions. 
Council will be asked to appoint two individuals to fill the remainder of the unexpired terms as 
follows: 
• Edward Ableser’s seat: April 22, 2026 – June 30, 2029 
• Kitty Jung’s seat: April 22, 2026 – June 30, 2027 
Staff are communicating with all applicants to keep them informed of the application timeline 
and the anticipated date of Council consideration.

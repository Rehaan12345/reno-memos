SUBJECT: Reno Seniors EngAGED Program Update 
In April 2023, the Reno City Council allocated funds from the American Rescue Plan Act 
(ARPA) for senior enrichment. The following is an overview of the free, senior-centered 
programming offered by Reno Senior EngAGED from September to December 2025. 
Date Event Participants 
September – December 2025 Weekly Line Dance Classes 828 
September - December 2025 Senior Art Classes  189 
September – December 2025 Fun Friday Dances 210 
October 9, & November 6, 2025 Senior Night at Dragon Lights 250 
October 15, 2025 Senior Day at the Automobile Museum 124 
October 23-24, 2025 Senior Tech Connect Classes 43 
December 4, 2025 Adopt-a-Senior 50 
December 11-12, 2025 Senior Tech Connect Classes 26 
Total Participants: 1,720 
ARPA funding for this program will conclude by the end of December 2026. At present, no 
additional funds are available to extend the programs beyond this date.

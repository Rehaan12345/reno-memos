SUBJECT: 2026 Waste Management Rate Increase Notice 
On March 6, 2026, Waste Management notified the City of Reno of rate adjustments in 
accordance with current residential and commercial agreements. The adjustments will take effect 
on April 1, 2026. 
Adjustments are based on the Consumer Index Fund (CPI) for garbage and trash collection. 
Increases take effect in accordance with Section 6.2 of the agreements and occur when Waste 
Management’s Return on Revenue (ROR) falls below ten percent (10%) on a three-year rolling 
average.  
The CPI increased by 5.27% from December 2024 to December 2025, resulting in a 5.27% 
increase to all service rates listed in the attached rate sheets.

Dear Ms. Bryant, 
This letter serves as formal notification of upcoming residential and commercial rate adjustments in 
accordance with the franchise agreements between the City of Reno and WM. These adjustments will 
take effect on April 1, 2026. 
Based on data published by the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, the Consumer Price Index (CPI) for 
Garbage and Trash Collection increased by 5.27% for the period from December 2024 through 
December 2025. 
In addition, following the City Council’s vote on February 11, 2026, to increase the franchise fee to 14%, 
WM has prepared updated calculations reflecting the combined impact of both the CPI adjustment and 
the revised franchise fee for residential and commercial services. 
To calculate the new rates, WM first removed the impact of the current 8% franchise fee from existing 
rates. The resulting base rate was then increased by the 5.27% CPI amount. Finally, the adjusted base 
rate was updated to reflect the new 14% franchise fee. 
Detailed calculations and updated rate tables are included in the following pages. 
Please feel free to contact me with any questions or if you would like to discuss these adjustments 
further.

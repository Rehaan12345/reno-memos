SUBJECT: Clean and Safe Team Homeless Actions and Outcomes – March 2026  
The City of Reno continues to address homeless-related concerns and provide outreach to the 
unsheltered community within city limits through the Clean and Safe Team. This 
interdepartmental team plays a critical role in addressing the multifaceted challenges and 
community impacts associated with homelessness, while coordinating multiple concurrent efforts 
and programs to ensure a comprehensive, citywide response.

March Updates 
• Reno Housing Authority Motel Assistance Program: In March, Clean and Safe  
Outreach facilitated 1 individual into the Motel Assistance program. One individual 
successfully transitioned out of the Motel Assistance Program into permanent housing.  
• Camera Mobile Trailers: Clean and Safe’s Mobile Camera Trailer Pilot Program is 
ongoing. The mobile trailers continue to keep previously chronic areas clear and serve as 
a deterrent. The trailers are currently installed at Spokane Street, along the railroad and 
Galletti Way, along the railroad.

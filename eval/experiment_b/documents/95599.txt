SUBJECT: 2026 Waste Management Allowance and Host Fees Increase Notice 
On February 27, 2026, Waste Management notified the City of Reno of rate adjustments in 
accordance with the City’s disposal agreement. The adjustments are based on the Consumer 
Price Index Fund (CPI) for garbage and trash collection, which increased 5.27% from December 
2024 to December 2025. The adjustments herein will take effect on April 1, 2026. 
In accordance with Section 3.3 of the agreement, City allowances will be increased as follows: 
Service Previous Allowance New Allowance Service explanation  
Collection $ 1,287,891.53 $ 1,355,763.41* Facility and receptacle collection 
Transfer Stations $ 1,249,970.47 $ 1,315,843.91 Stead and Sage St. drop-off  
Lockwood Landfill $ 60,974.17 $ 64,187.51 Lockwood drop-off 
* Additional funding for clean-up boxes is $20,118.32  
In accordance with Section 3.6 of the agreement, the host fee paid by Waste Management to the 
City of Reno will increase from $0.63 to $0.66 per ton.  
In accordance with Section 5.2 of the agreement, the following rates are not paid by the public 
and are paid by Waste Management post-collections, but will also increase: 
 Current Rate Rate Change New Rate 
Compacted Yards $ 17.30 $ 0.91 $ 18.21 
Uncompacted Yards $ 13.50 $ 0.71 $ 14.21 
Approved Recyclables (tons) $ 65.58 $ 3.46 $ 69.04

This is to notify you of rate adjustments per the franchise agreements between the City of Reno and 
WM. Per the Bureau of Labor Statistics the Consumer Price Index-Garbage and Trash Collection for the 
period of December 2024 to December 2025 increased 5.27%. The following changes will take effect 
April 1, 2026. 
Per Section 3.6 of the Disposal Agreement, the Host Fee WM pays to the City of Reno will increase from 
$.63 per ton to $.66 per ton for all material passing through transfer stations in Reno.  
Per section 5.2 of the Disposal Agreement, solid waste rates per ton will be adjusted as listed below. 
These are internal rates between the hauling company and post collections. It does not affect the rate 
paid by the public at the transfer station.

SUBJECT: Reno Seniors EngAGED Quarterly Update 
In April 2023, the Reno City Council approved a senior enrichment American Rescue Plan Act 
(ARPA) allocation to help combat senior isolation post the pandemic. The following is a 
summary of Reno Senior EngAGED free, senior centered programming for January – March 
2026.   
Date Event  
Participants 
January – March 2026 Weekly Line Dance Classes 664 
January – March 2026 Senior Art Classes  232 
January 22, 2026 Senior Resource Fair 360 
Total Participants: 1,256 
In accordance with ARPA regulations, funding for these programs will conclude at the end of 
December 2026.

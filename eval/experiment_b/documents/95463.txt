SUBJECT: December 2025 Clean & Safe Team Homeless Actions & Outcomes  
The City of Reno continues to address homeless-related concerns and provide outreach to the 
unsheltered community within city limits through the Clean and Safe Team. This 
interdepartmental team plays a critical role in addressing the multifaceted challenges and 
community impacts associated with homelessness, while coordinating multiple concurrent efforts 
and programs to ensure a comprehensive, citywide response.

SUBJECT: Annual update on Projects listed in the Regional Transportation Commission  
  Interlocal Cooperative Agreements for Fiscal Years 2020 through 2026 
This memo provides Council with an update on the current work phase and anticipated 
construction schedule for projects approved through Interlocal Cooperative Agreements (ICA) 
with the Regional Transportation Commission (RTC) for Fiscal Years 2020 to 2026.  This memo 
highlights the projects in various design phases, and the full list of all projects is included in the 
attachment.  
 
The RTC’s Regional Transportation Plan (RTP) is a 20-year guiding document that includes 
short-term and long-term strategies to foster development of an integrated multi-modal regional 
transportation system that facilitates the safe and efficient movement of people and goods. Next, 
the RTC’s 5-year Regional Transportation Improvement Plan (RTIP) is developed from the short-
term list of projects and programs, and then ICAs are developed. 
 
Since the RTC does not own any roads, in accordance with state law, an ICA is required to 
outline the responsibilities for delivering new projects within the City's jurisdiction and to 
authorize the RTC to exercise eminent domain if necessary. 
 
These ICAs are essential for advancing the tasks outlined in these programs. The RTC drafts 
separate agreements for each jurisdiction, based on the project’s location. This approach provides 
clarity to the overarching project program and facilitates the independent approval of each ICA 
and ensuring that delays in one jurisdiction does not impede the approval process in another.

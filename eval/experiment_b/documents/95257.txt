SUBJECT: City of Reno Boards and Commissions 2025 Q3 V acancy Report 
This memo provides an overview of current vacancies on the City of Reno’s boards and 
commissions, as well as those anticipated within the next 90 days as of January 1, 2026. Changes 
in membership occurring after that date are not reflected.  
The scope of this report is limited to internal boards and commissions for which appointments 
are made directly by the Reno City Council. Its purpose is to assist the Mayor and Council in 
planning for upcoming appointments and reappointments, particularly to the boards where they 
serve as Council liaisons. To further support this process, staff will also meet individually with 
each Council Member throughout the quarter to review vacancies and applications for the boards 
they recommend appointments to. These meetings will help ensure timely appointments and 
reappointments as vacancies occur.

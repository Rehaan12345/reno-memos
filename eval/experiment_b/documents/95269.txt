SUBJECT: Monthly Rental and Deposit Assistance Program Update – December 2025 
During the March 31, 2022 City Council Special Meeting, staff was asked to provide monthly 
updates on rental and deposit assistance provided through the Housing and Neighborhood 
Development (HAND) department. The following information reflects the amount expended and 
number of unique households assisted with rental, deposit, and/or utility assistance.  
December 2025 
• $354,481.96 expended  
• 141 unique households assisted  
Expenditures for December 2025 were 1% more than November 2025 ($353,399.75). From July 
1, 2025 to date, $1,572,111.85 has been expended through the rental and deposit assistance 
program.

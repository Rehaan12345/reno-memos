SUBJECT:  City of Reno Boards and Commissions 2026 Q4 V acancy Report 
This memo is intended to provide an overview of current vacancies on the City of Reno’s boards 
and commissions, as well as vacancies anticipated within the next 90 days as of April 1, 2026. 
Changes in membership occurring after that date are not reflected.  
The scope of this report is limited to internal boards and commissions for which appointments 
are made directly by the Reno City Council. Its purpose is to assist the Mayor and City Council 
in planning for upcoming appointments and reappointments, particularly to the boards for which 
they serve as Council liaisons. To further support this process, staff will also meet individually 
with each Council Member throughout the quarter to review vacancies and applications for the 
boards they recommend appointments to. These meetings will help ensure timely appointments 
and reappointments as vacancies occur.

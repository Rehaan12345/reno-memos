SUBJECT: Reno Direct & Service Requests Report – February 2026 
Monthly reports provide insights into how constituents engage with the City through Reno 
Direct, the City’s centralized customer service center and ServiceNow platform.  
Key report highlights include: 
• Number of Reno Direct constituent interactions (calls, voicemails, emails, online chats) 
• Number of service requests submitted citywide 
• Number of service requests closed citywide 
• Top five trending service request types citywide  
• Top five trending service request types by ward 
This information supports efforts to enhance service delivery while addressing community needs 
in a responsive and equitable manner.

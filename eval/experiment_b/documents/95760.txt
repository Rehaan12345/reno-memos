SUBJECT:  City Walk Outcomes – March 2026 
The City Walks Program is designed to enhance quality of life for residents, neighborhoods, and 
commercial areas, while expediting service delivery to vulnerable populations.  
Coordinated by Clean and Safe, City Walks are data-driven and strategically aligned with 
broader organizational initiatives. Uses an interdepartmental approach to promote proactive 
problem-solving, address issues early, collaborate with community members, and coordinate 
with departmental initiatives to connect residents, businesses, services, and enforcement efforts. 
Walks take place four days per week, Monday to Thursday, including two days dedicated to 
walks within the Business Improvement District (BID) in partnership with the Downtown Reno 
Partnership. 
City Walks Departments 
• Clean and Safe – Community Engagement and Services  
• Code Enforcement – Licensing and Code Enforcement  
• Park and River Rangers – Parks and Recreation (Based on locations) 
• Community Action & Outreach (CAO), Mobile Engagement Team (MET), and Parking 
Enforcement – Reno Police
